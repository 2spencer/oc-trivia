About this guide

Keep the list honest

Trivia schedules change constantly — venues swap nights, hosts go on hiatus during big sports seasons, and new spots pop up without much fanfare.

This guide covers San Clemente, Dana Point, San Juan Capistrano, Laguna Beach, Laguna Niguel, Mission Viejo, Rancho Santa Margarita, and surrounding South OC communities.

Each listing shows the last known schedule. Always confirm with the venue's Instagram before heading out — it's the most reliable real-time source for cancellations and theme nights.

Know something we don't?

If your local spot runs trivia and isn't listed — or if a schedule here is out of date — let us know. We update this list weekly.
